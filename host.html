How it Works


1 Sign up to host



Listing Information

You will fill out general information about your listing, provide a description, upload photos, set house rules, and pick a price.



Who can book a listing?

You will set availability and house rules for your listing. Only confirmed guests will be able to see your exact location.

2 Guests search for your listing and book



Booking notification

When a guest books your listings you will be notified by email. Potential guests can also email with questions regarding the listing.



How to guests pay?

Guests are required to pay by credit card upon booking. Refunds vary according to StayMarta's Cancellation Policy.

3 Welcome your new guests



Plan for your quests

Coordinate with your guest regarding logistics such as key swapping, parking, and alarms.



How do hosts get paid?

You will be paid once the guest checks-in. Guests pay a 10% service fee and StayMarta takes 3% of the total transaction.
